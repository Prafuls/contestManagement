Directions to Dulles High School

North: From US 59 North, exit onto US Alt. 90, turn left onto 90 East and take the first exit to Dulles Ave. Turn right on Dulles Ave. to arrive in front parking lot.
South: From US 59 South, exit onto US Alt. 90, turn right onto 90 East and take the first exit to Dulles Ave. Turn right on Dulles Ave. to arrive in front parking lot.
West: From US Alt. 90 West, continue east and take the exit to Dulles Ave. and Kirkwood Rd. Turn right on Dulles Ave. to arrive in front parking lot.
East: From US Alt. 90 East, continue west and take the exit to Dulles Ave. and Kirkwood Rd. Turn left on Dulles Ave. to arrive in front parking lot.
